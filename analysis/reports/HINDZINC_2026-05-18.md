INVESTMENT THESIS
  # Hindustan Zinc (HINDZINC) – Investment Thesis

Hindustan Zinc's 75% domestic market share, anchored by the world's largest underground zinc mine and vertically integrated operations with captive power, creates a durable cost-leadership moat that generates industry-leading EBITDA margins of 46–57% and ROCE of ~70%—a structural advantage nearly impossible for new entrants to replicate given regulatory barriers and resource endowment. India's rapid infrastructure and automotive growth will drive zinc demand expansion, and HINDZINC's historical 5-year revenue CAGR of 13% demonstrates its ability to translate volume growth into cash generation. While valuation is marginally above our buy-zone threshold (MoS of -3.8%), the company's oligopoly position, pricing power, and capital-light operational model offer an attractive asymmetric risk-reward for a 5–10 year compounder, particularly on any near-term commodity or demand softness.
